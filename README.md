# Baby-ProductOrderForm

Welcome to the order request system for **A Lil' Something by Lo**! 

### 📋 How to Place Your Pre-Order

1. **Open the Form:** Click the link below to open the interactive form directly in your web browser:
   👉 [**Click Here to Open the Order Request Form**](https://alilsomethingbylo.github.io/Baby-ProductOrderForm
E/asbl_order_request_form.html)

2. **Fill It Out:** Type in your shipping info, choose your quantities, and enter your customization details online.

3. **Download Your Copy:** Scroll to the bottom and click the **"Save & Download Form"** button. A completed copy of the form will instantly download to your device.

4. **Submit:** Attach that downloaded file to an email and send it to **alilsomethingbylo@gmail.com**. We will review your choices and send you an invoice shortly!
